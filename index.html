Frequently Asked Questions

Event design encompasses the more creative side of event management. While a planner focuses on organization, we focus on the decor of the event. From draping to lighting, furniture, and centerpieces, it is our job to ensure your event is truly stunning.

Planning an event can take from days to weeks. It really depends on the size and the details that the event involves.

With our experience, planning private and corporate events requires a shorter period of time because guests usually have already confirmed their attendance, and no details are needed to be released to the public. The only things that need planning are the event elements such as catering, entertainment, technical and venue.

All cancellation requests must be made in writing. An 85% refund will be granted when the cancellation request is received 5 business days prior to the scheduled event. A 50% refund will be granted if the request is received between 2 and 3 business days prior to the scheduled event. No refunds will be granted if the request is received fewer than 1 day prior to the scheduled event.

1. We begin planning as soon as we possibly can.

2. Remember that with every event there will be unforeseen costs, so we try to negotiate as low a price as we can.

3. We Break up the various elements of the event into sections and assign a section to each member of our team. As we are solely responsible for our own section we will be much more clued into small detail changes.

4. We assess the most important assets your event will have, and we create a backup plan for each.

5. About two days before the event, we fixed a meeting with you and share with you everything, from the initial setup to the follow-up process.
